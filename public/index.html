Handmade pizza
for everyday life

At Andrew Pizzeria, we believe great pizza shouldn't be reserved for special occasions. Our dough is made fresh daily, our sauce simmers slow, and every pie leaves our stone ovens with the kind of char and chew that makes a Tuesday night feel like a celebration.

Stone-baked taste

Our 700°F stone decks lock in moisture and deliver the leopard-spotted crust that defines true artisan pizza. No shortcuts, no conveyor belts — just fire, flour, and patience.

Built for busy nights

Order ahead through our app and your pizza is hot and ready when you pull up. We've designed every step — from dough ball to pickup window — to respect your time without compromising quality.

Rooted in Oneida

150 Madison Street is where it started, but our kitchen has grown to serve families across the region. We're proud to be a local brand that neighbors count on, whether dining in or grabbing dinner to go.
